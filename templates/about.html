Our Story

Built by students, for students.

How It Started

We're college students, just like you. During move-out week, we watched perfectly good couches, mini-fridges, and furniture pile up in dumpsters. We were throwing our own stuff out too—and we thought: why can't we make money on this?

So we did something about it. We built Campus Swap to give students a better way to handle move-out. Instead of dragging your stuff to the curb, you snap a photo. We handle pickup, storage, and selling—and you get 40% when your item sells.

We're not a big corporation. We're students who saw a problem and built a solution. We know what it's like to move out of a dorm, and we know what incoming students need. That's why Campus Swap works.

Where We're Growing

We started at Davidson College and saw how much students loved the service. Now we're expanding to UNC and beyond.

Our Mission

Campus Swap exists because we believe move-out doesn't have to be wasteful. We believe students deserve to get paid for their stuff, and incoming students deserve affordable furniture.

We're students helping students. That's it. No corporate speak, no complicated process. Just a simple way to turn your move-out items into cash.
