Snap & Relax.

Let’s face it: you’re not going to sell your mini-fridge before move-out. Don’t waste time on Facebook Marketplace. Snap a photo of your furniture and appliances, and we’ll handle the rest.

Leave in May. Get Paid All Summer.

You leave in May. Buyers don’t. Students arrive all summer and into next semester, and they need what you’re leaving behind. We pick up your items, list them at our store near campus, and sell them 24/7 for three months. No dumping. No waiting around. We handle everything.

Better Than Storage. Smarter Than Facebook.

A storage unit costs you money all summer with nothing to show for it. Facebook Marketplace means strangers, no-shows, and hauling stuff yourself. With us, you make money instead of spending it — and we do all the heavy lifting.
