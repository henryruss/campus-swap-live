Seller FAQ

What items can I sell?
  • Larger items that typically sell for ~$50+
  • Examples: couches, mini fridges, mattresses, futons, AC units, TVs
  • No small or low-value items
What happens if my item doesn't sell?
  • In the unlikely event that your item doesn’t sell, we donate it to a local Goodwill or Habitat for Humanity store
When will you pick up my items?
  • We organize pickups by area during move-out
  • You’ll get an email 1-2 days before with your window
  • We’ll text or call 30-60 minutes before we arrive
Do I need to be home for pickup?
  • Yes. Someone must be home to let our team in
  • We’ll contact you before we arrive
What if I’m not home when you arrive?
  • We’ll call you
  • If you can’t get home quickly, we’ll try to reschedule once within the pickup period
